# Maintainers

HoneybeePF is maintained by the honeybee-studio core team. Maintainers have
commit rights and are responsible for project direction, code review, releases,
and final decisions on proposals.

## Becoming a Maintainer

Active contributors who consistently review and merge work across multiple
releases may be invited to join the maintainer group. See [GOVERNANCE.md](GOVERNANCE.md)
for details.
